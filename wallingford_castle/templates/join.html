Join the club

Wallingford Castle Archers is an active club with around 150 members. We cater for all archers - whether you are 6 or 106, a complete beginner or an olympic athlete.

We shoot all through the year with some shooting taking place almost every day of the year. In the outdoor season, serious archers can also access 24 hour shooting facilities.

Coaching

We run a wide range of group coaching programs for archers of all ages and levels. We run groups for novices, intermediates and performance adults, as well as two levels of junior groups and minis sessions. For most groups we run sessions on two different days in the week to maximise availability for everyone, although archers typically only attend one.

Adult coaching groups are £25/month and junior coaching groups are £20/month, both paid monthly with membership. Minis sessions are £4/week and are booked in blocks by term.

Membership types

Adult members
£20 per calendar month. Membership includes the Sunday afternoon sessions, use of club equipment and membership to the national governing body, Archery GB. All new members must commit to at least 6 months of membership to cover their affiliation fees.

Concession members
£15 per calendar month. Concession membership is available to all archers aged 6-24, and any archers with a disability. Membership includes sessions, including a junior only session on Sunday mornings, use of club equipment and membership to the national governing body, Archery GB. All new members must commit to at least 3 months of membership.

Archers aged 6-11 (attending primary school) will be part of the minis minis club on a Sunday morning which has additional session charges. We also have links to a number of local schools and leisure centre groups.

Associate members
£15 per calendar month. Membership includes everything that full membership does, with the exception of membership to Archery GB. Membership is due monthly. You must already be a member of Archery GB or another World Archery affiliated body.

Non-shooting members
£5 per calendar month. Membership includes only membership of Archery GB, with no shooting rights.
