Join the club

Wallingford Castle Archers is an active club with over 80 members. We cater for all archers - whether you are 6 or 106, a complete beginner or an olympic athlete.

We shoot all through the year with two sessions a week during the indoor season and three club sessions during the outdoor season. In the outdoor season, serious archers can also access 24 hour shooting facilities.

Coaching

We run regular boost courses for intermediate archers - ideal to develop your skills beyond what you learn on your beginners course.

All junior sessions are supported by qualified coaches, and minis sessions are led by a qualified coach.

Tailored individual coaching programs are available to all club members and visiting archers.

Membership types

Adult members
£15 per calendar month. Membership includes the Sunday afternoon sessions, use of club equipment and membership to the national governing body, Archery GB. All new members must commit to at least 6 months of membership to cover their affiliation fees.

Concession members
£10 per calendar month. Concession membership is available to all archers aged 6-24, and any archers with a disability. Membership includes sessions, including a junior only session on Sunday mornings, use of club equipment and membership to the national governing body, Archery GB. All new members must commit to at least 3 months of membership.

Archers aged 6-11 (attending primary school) will be part of the minis minis club on a Sunday morning which has additional session charges. We also have links to a number of local schools and leisure centre groups.

Associate members
£10 per calendar month. Membership includes everything that full membership does, with the exception of membership to Archery GB. Membership is due monthly. You must already be a member of Archery GB or another World Archery affiliated body.

Non-shooting members
£5 per calendar month. Membership includes only membership of Archery GB, with no shooting rights.
