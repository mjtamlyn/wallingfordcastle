Join the club

Wallingford Castle Archers is an active club with over 200 members. We cater for all archers - whether you are 6 or 106, a complete beginner or an olympic athlete.

We shoot all through the year with some shooting taking place at least 5 days a week.

Coaching

We run a wide range of group coaching programs for archers of all ages and levels. We run groups for novices, intermediates and performance adults, as well as several levels of junior groups, and minis sessions. For most groups we run sessions on at least two different days in the week to maximise availability for everyone, although archers typically only attend one.

Adult coaching groups are £30/month and junior coaching groups are £25/month, both paid monthly with membership. We also have more advanced junior groups which meet more regularly for those who wish to take competitive archery seriously. Minis sessions are £5/week and are booked in blocks by term.

Archery GB Membership

All club members must be members of Archery GB. You are responsible for keeping your own membership up to date, and it is required for insurance purposes.

Membership of Archery GB is not sufficient to guarantee acceptance as a club member. New archers should complete a beginners course first, returning archers should contact the club coaches to arrange an assessment.

Membership types

Adult members
£20 per calendar month. Membership includes open practice sessions and use of club equipment.

Concession members
£17 calendar month. Concession membership is available to all archers aged 6-20, and any archers with a disability. Membership includes open sessions, use of club equipment.

Archers aged 6-11 (attending primary school) will be part of the minis club on Sundays or Mondays which has no standing monthly charge, but weekly session charges.
