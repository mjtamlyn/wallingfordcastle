About

We are a club based in Wallingford, Oxfordshire, with a focus on promoting a friendly club with a competitive edge. As well as running regular beginners courses for both adults and children, our members compete regularly on a club, county and national level. Archery is a highly inclusive sport, with juniors able to learn along side grandparents, and able bodied alongside disabled.

Get into archery

Beginners courses are the ideal introduction to the sport for adults and secondary school age children. You'll you all the basics including how to set up, shoot the bow and score your arrows.

Find out more

For younger children, we offer a minis club from 6 years old. We have weekly structured sessions in term time with plenty opportunities to earn their beginners badges.
