About

We are a club based in Wallingford, Oxfordshire, with a focus on promoting a friendly club with a competitive edge. As well as running regular beginners courses for both adults and children, our members compete regularly on a club, county and national level. Archery is a highly inclusive sport, with juniors able to learn along side grandparents, and able bodied alongside disabled.

News

First club session will be Sunday 11th October!

Our first beginners course of the season will start on Sunday 11th October and run every Sunday at 10am until Sunday 1st November. We will be running a have a go at the school on Saturday 3rd October, so come along and try your hand at our sport if you think archery might be for you.

Session times

Our sessions run from 10am to 4pm every Sunday.

  • 10:00-11:30 – Beginners course or alternative coaching session
  • 11:30-13:00 – Junior session. Only junior members will be allowed to shoot, along side any parents, space permitting.
  • 13:00-16:00 – Open session. All members welcome, but under 18s must be supervised by an adult.

Location

Both our indoor and outdoor ranges are based at Wallingford School, on the main sports field from April to September, and the the Castle Sports Hall from October to April.

Membership

Adult members
£15 per calendar month. Membership includes the Sunday afternoon sessions, use of club equipment and membership to the national governing body, Archery GB. All new members must commit to at least 6 months of membership to cover their affiliation fees.

Junior members and students
£10 per calendar month. Membership includes sessions, including a junior only session on Sunday mornings, as well as the usual open afternoon session, use of club equipment and membership to the national governing body, Archery GB. All new members must commit to at least 3 months of membership.

Associate members
£10 per calendar month. Membership includes everything that full membership does, with the exception of membership to Archery GB. Membership is due monthly. You must already be a member of Archery GB or another World Archery affiliated body.

Beginners

All courses will be four sessions of 1½ hours per session. All equipment and coaching will be provided, and at the end of the course you will be given a short assessment, just to make sure you've taken what we've taught you. After this you will be able to join any other open archery club in the country. The course, including hire of bows etc. for the full 4 sessions will cost £50 for adults and £30 for juniors.

If you would like to sign up for the next beginners course, please fill in the form below. The course have limited numbers due to the size of the hall and availability of targets, and places will be allocated on a first come first served basis where possible. Payment accepted by cash or cheque (payable to Wallingford Castle Archers) at the first session, or by bank transfer.
