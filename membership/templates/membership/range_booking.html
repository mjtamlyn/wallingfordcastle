Range bookings

To book a session, select an upcoming club day and find an available slot. You can then select the archer(s) who will be attending and set a distance.

Open indoor sessions are available on Fridays and Sundays. Please do not book these sessions until the day of shooting if you are in a training group, and only book one session per week. Our priority will be to make sure that all members get to shoot once per week.

We have open outdoor shooting on Saturday afternoons. These sessions are not subject to the same restrictions as Fridays and Sundays, but you must have your own equipment.
