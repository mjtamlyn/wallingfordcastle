Range bookings

To book a session, select an upcoming club day and find an available slot. You can then select the archer(s) who will be attending.

Unless you have been told otherwise, you may not shoot if you are the only person on the range. Feel free to book a slot when no one else is shooting yet, but be aware it may be cancelled on the day if no one else is shooting at that time.

Junior Archers who have achieved their white arrow award and have their own equipment may attend open shooting slots, but they need to have only one non-shooting family member present on the range, who must wear a face covering at all times. Junior members can also be supervised by a coach or shooting parent. Please note parents are still not allowed to attend junior group sessions.
