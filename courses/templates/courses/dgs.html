Didcot Girls School

Wallingford Castle Archers are pleased to be working with Didcot Girls School to offer an after school archery club. We meet on Monday after school, on the school field when weather permits, and then in Frids Gym during the winter.

Sessions will be running after school on Mondays on the following dates:

  • 9th September - Archers with prior experience only
  • 16th September - First session for new archers
  • 23rd September
  • 30th September
  • 7th October
  • 14th October
  • 21st October
  • No session half term - 28th October
  • 4th November
  • 11th November
  • 18th November
  • 25th November
  • 2nd December
  • 9th December
  • 16th December

The cost for this term is £65 for new archers, £70 for existing archers, payable in advance.
