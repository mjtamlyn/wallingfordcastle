COVID notice

All sessions will be run in accordance with government rules and Archery GB COVID-secure guidelines. Children are expected to do their best to maintain social distancing, but we understand that it is difficult for the youngest archers to do so. No masks are required, and cannot be worn when shooting. Parents and guardians may stay on site, but should be at their vehicles in the car park (within view of the range), or they may leave children in our care. Please ensure that adults maintain social distancing at all times, and that any gatherings of adults or older siblings comply with current government guidance.
